All what you want to know about Verba

At Verba, we believe, that this site can make learning of words easy.

Verba was created for every people, who wants to achieve and grow using new instruments and ways.

We believe, that Verba will help not only people, who want to learn foreign language. Native speakers also can find
it useful to expand their vocabulary.

This site is only beginning of great work ahead. We want to grow together with you. That's why we will appreciate
every comment about our work and every suggestion, how to make our Verba better.


Our aim is to become one of the leading resource for learning worldwide by staying focused on the goals and principles
set forth when forming Verba:

    Our goals:
  • Expand access to education for everyone
  • Enhance learning online
  • Advance learning through new technologies
    Our principles:
  • Nonprofit
  • Open source platform
  • Collaborative

Verba is based in Kazan, Russian Federation
